Welcome to Smt. Radhika Devi Intermediate College

This recognized Smt. Radhika Devi Intermediate College was founded in November 2011 at Mathauli bazar, kushinagar by revered Mr. Narendra Srivastav, but now this esteemed educational institute is spearding the flame of knowledge at Mathauli Bazar, Kushinagar. This is a Co-Educational school affiliated to the U.P.M.S.P., Prayagraj, where boyes and girl both get futurist Education.

Mission Of Smt. Radhika Devi Intermediate College

To provide a conducive environment for a future-oriented, holistic education, anchored in a rigorous bilingual academic programme, for children of all nationalities. We nurture students to be independent life-long learners imbued with sound moral values who are respectful and responsible, and who rise to the challenges of life creatively and enthusiastically in an ever-changing world.
