General Instruction
1. All students must be present in school on the opening day of each term.

2. No books other than the required study materials may be brought to the school. No student should bring to the school objectionable articles or electronic goods such as camera, tape-recorder, knives, weapons, crackers, explosives etc. The offenders are liable to be suspended or restricate from the school and the confiscated goods will not be returned. The decision of the school authorities is binding to all.

3. Any damage to school property is will have to be made good. The decision of the Principal regarding the amount is final.

4. During school hours the students are not allowed to receive visitors or make phone calls.

5. Every pupil must endeavour to keep up high morale of the institution by excelling in good manners and courtesy and by fostering good family spirit among the fellow pupils.

6. While going to the labs, library playground, all should move in an orderly manner under the supervision of their teachers.

7. The students are not allowed to enter a classroom other than their own.

8. Articles found in school or on its premises are to be handed over to the office.

9. Principalâ€™s Permission is required in the following -
a) To make a collection for any purpose whatever.
b) To arrange for a party, picnic or meeting.
c) To join sports or to play a game not arranged by the school.
d) To give presents to teachers or stage demonstration in their honour.

10. Long finger nails, nail polish, Mehandi, the wearing of finger nails, chains or any kind of jewellery are strictly prohibited.

11. Buying sweets, ice-cream, snacks, etc. from the hawkers on the street outside the school premises is discouraged.

Pupils are strictly forbidden to come to school on mopeds, scooters, motorbikes, etc

12. If any student is found using the following unfair means will be given zero in the subject. Repetition of the same will result in dismissal.
a) Having in possession papers, notes or any other material or information to the examination in the paper concerned.
b) Giving or receiving assistance directly or indirectly of any kind or attempting to do so.
c) Writing question or answer on any other material other than the answer book.

13. Gifts to the members of the staff or other demonstrations in their honour, require the previous sanction of the Principal. Gifts from parents to teachers or demonstrations in their honour are not allowed. (repeated)

14. All pupils are expected to speak in the medium of instruction, i.e. English in the school campus. This is for the benefit of the pupils academically.(Repeated)

15. Pupils are responsible for the safer custody of all their books and other belongings while at school.

16. Pupils are not to wear/bring valuables like gold ring/chains or any other jewelry inside the school campus. Hence the school disclaims all responsibility for such articles.
